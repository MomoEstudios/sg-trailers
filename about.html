Since 2019, SG Trailers has been manufacturing professional-grade car haulers in Yuma, Arizona. We're not here to compete on price—we're here to build the trailers that professionals trust for decades.

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

SG Trailers was founded on a simple principle: the hauling industry deserved better. Better engineering. Better materials. Better service. We started with a 12,000 sq ft facility in Yuma, Arizona, and a commitment to build every trailer as if our reputation depended on it—because it does.

Today, we manufacture four distinct trailer lines, each designed for a specific segment of the market. From entry-level operators to seasoned professionals hauling high-value cargo, our trailers are built to handle whatever the road throws at them.
