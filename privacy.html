Privacy Policy

How SG Trailers collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Short version: We collect your contact information only to respond to your inquiries about our trailers. We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.

1. Who We Are

SG Trailers LLC ("SG Trailers", "we", "us", or "our") is a direct trailer manufacturer based in Yuma, Arizona. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information collected through our website sgtrailers.com and any related pages.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: info@sgtrailers.com

2. What Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our forms:

Contact & Quote Forms

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Message or inquiry details
  • Trailer model of interest

Dealer Application Form

  • Full name and business name
  • Email address and phone number
  • City, state, and country
  • Business details (years in business, territory, etc.)

Configurator / Build Your Trailer

  • Name, email, and phone number (only when submitting a quote request)
  • Trailer configuration selections (model, size, options)

Email Notifications (Parts & Accessories)

  • Email address only (to notify when parts store launches)

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, government IDs, or any sensitive financial data through this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry or quote request
  • To process dealer program applications
  • To send product updates or promotions — only if you explicitly opted in
  • To improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. How We Store Your Information

Form submissions are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a secure form processing service. Your data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Formspree stores submissions on your behalf and may retain them for up to 30 days depending on the plan. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not operate our own database or server that stores your personal information long-term.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses localStorage (browser storage) to remember your display preference (light or dark mode). This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers
  • Contains no personal information
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies at this time.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — to load typography. Google may log font requests. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Three.js (jsDelivr CDN) — for 3D model rendering. No personal data is shared.
  • Formspree — for processing form submissions.

7. Your Rights (CCPA — California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know — Request information about what personal data we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Opt-Out — We do not sell data, so this right is already exercised
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not treat you differently for exercising your rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@sgtrailers.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 45 days.

8. Marketing Communications

We only send promotional emails or product updates if you explicitly checked the opt-in box on one of our forms. You can unsubscribe at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any email we send
  • Emailing us at info@sgtrailers.com with subject "Unsubscribe"

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: info@sgtrailers.com
  • Phone: +1 (760) 000-0000
  • Address: SG Trailers LLC, Yuma, Arizona, USA
