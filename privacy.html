Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Short version: We collect your contact information only when you reach out about our trailers. We do not sell your data to anyone — ever. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

01

Who We Are

SG Trailers LLC is a direct trailer manufacturer based in Yuma, Arizona. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information collected through our website and any related pages.

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@sgtrailers.com

02

What We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our forms. We do not collect payment info, government IDs, or financial data.

Contact & Quote Forms

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Message or inquiry details
  • Trailer model of interest

Dealer Application

  • Name, business name, email, phone
  • City, state, country
  • Business details (territory, years in business)

Build Your Trailer (Configurator)

  • Name, email, phone — only when requesting a quote
  • Trailer configuration selections (model, size, options)

Parts & Accessories Notify

  • Email address only
03

How We Use It

  • To respond to your inquiry or quote request
  • To process dealer applications
  • To send product updates — only if you opted in
  • To improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

04

How We Store It

Form submissions are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. Formspree may retain submissions for up to 30 days. We do not operate our own database storing your personal data long-term.

See Formspree's Privacy Policy →

05

Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage only to remember your light/dark display preference. This data stays on your device, is never sent to our servers, contains no personal information, and is cleared when you clear your browser data.

We do not use tracking cookies, ad cookies, or third-party analytics at this time.

06

Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts — for typography. Google may log font requests. Google Privacy Policy →
  • jsDelivr CDN — delivers Three.js for 3D preview. No personal data shared.
  • Formspree — processes form submissions.
07

Your Rights — CCPA

If you are a California resident (or purchasing from near the California border), you have these rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

Right to Know

Request what personal data we have collected about you.

Right to Delete

Request deletion of your personal data from our records.

Right to Opt-Out

We do not sell data — this right is already protected.

Non-Discrimination

We will never treat you differently for exercising your rights.

To submit a request, email info@sgtrailers.com with subject "Privacy Request". We respond within 45 days.

08

Marketing Emails

We only send promotional content if you explicitly checked the opt-in box on one of our forms. You can unsubscribe any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email, or by emailing info@sgtrailers.com with subject "Unsubscribe".

09

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their information. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, contact us immediately.

10

Policy Changes

We may update this policy over time. When we do, we update the date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing it periodically if you've submitted a form.
