The Process

How It Works

Financing your SG Trailer is straightforward. Your local dealer handles the process from application to approval — we just build the best trailer possible.

🚛
01
Choose Your Trailer

Browse our four models and find the one that matches your work. Whether you're hauling one car or a fleet, we have the right trailer at the right price point.

🤝
02
Connect with a Dealer

Your local SG Trailers dealer will walk you through available financing programs, current rates, and documentation requirements. They manage the entire application process.

🔑
03
Get Approved & Drive Away

Once approved, finalize your paperwork at the dealership and drive away with your new SG Trailer. It's that simple — no factory delays, no runaround.

Important Note on Financing

SG Trailers LLC does not directly provide financing or act as a lender. All financing is arranged independently through our authorized dealer network and their lending partners. Rates, terms, and availability vary by location, dealer, and individual credit profile. Contact your nearest SG Trailers dealer for current financing options in your area.
