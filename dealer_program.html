Why Partner With Us

The Dealer Advantage

We build the trailers, you sell them. It's that simple. Our dealer program is designed to maximize your profit with minimum friction.

💰
Competitive Margins
Industry-leading dealer margins on every unit. The more you sell, the better your pricing gets. Volume tiers available starting at 5 units.
📍
Territory Exclusivity
We protect your investment. Approved dealers receive exclusive rights within their geographic area so you're not competing against other SG dealers.
🏭
Direct From Factory
No middlemen, no distributors. You buy directly from us at factory prices, giving you maximum flexibility on your retail pricing.
📦
Marketing Support
We provide professional photography, spec sheets, brochures and digital assets for all models. You focus on selling, we handle the materials.
🤝
Dedicated Support
Direct line to our factory team. Technical questions, custom orders, warranty claims — handled fast with a real person, not a call center.
📈
Growing Product Line
Four models today, more coming. As our lineup grows, your inventory options grow with it. Be part of a brand that is actively expanding.
The Process

How It Works

Getting started as an SG Trailers dealer is straightforward. Here's what to expect from application to first sale.

01
Submit Your Application
Fill out the form below with your business details, location and estimated sales volume. We review every application personally within 48 hours.
02
Discovery Call
A member of our team will contact you to discuss territory availability, pricing tiers, and answer any questions about the program. No pressure, just a real conversation.
03
Place Your First Order
Once approved, place your first order of 5 units minimum to activate your territory exclusivity. Your dealer account is set up and you're ready to sell.
What We Look For

Dealer Requirements

We're selective about our dealer network because our reputation depends on it. Here's what we typically look for in a partner.

🏢
Established Business
Active business entity (LLC, Corp, or Sole Prop) with at least 1 year of operation in automotive, equipment or related industry.
📏
Physical Location
Display space to showcase at least 2-3 units. Customers want to see trailers in person before buying.
🔢
Minimum Order
Initial order of 5 units to activate territory exclusivity. Subsequent orders have no minimum.
🌎
Territory Availability
Territory must not already be covered by an existing SG dealer. We'll confirm availability during the discovery call.
