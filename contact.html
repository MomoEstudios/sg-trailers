Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.
Do you sell directly to individuals? +
Yes, absolutely. We sell directly from the factory to individual buyers — no dealers required. This means you get factory pricing without any markup.
How long does it take to build my trailer? +
Standard configurations typically take 2–4 weeks from order to delivery. Custom specifications may take 4–6 weeks depending on complexity and current production schedule.
Can I order a custom size or configuration? +
Yes. We can build to your specifications within reasonable parameters. Contact us with your requirements and we'll confirm feasibility and pricing during a discovery call.
What warranty comes with my trailer? +
Warranty varies by model — from 1 year structural on the Apolo up to 5 years full structural on the Apocalypse. All models include a 1-year warranty on electrical components.
Do you offer financing? +
Yes. We work with multiple financing institutions to offer flexible terms — from 12 to 84 months, with options for 0% down for qualified buyers. Pre-approval typically takes 24 hours.
Can I become a dealer? +
Yes — we're actively expanding our dealer network. Minimum first order is 5 units with exclusive territory rights. Visit our Dealer Program page to apply or contact us directly.
